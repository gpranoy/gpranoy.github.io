Learning experience design - Better By Design

About Better by design

Better by Design is a startup in Hyderabad. Our goal was to build and sustain the creative confidence of students (of class 7-10).

Role

I worked as a designer & researcher responsible for immersive learning experiences. I played a pivotal role in creating game-like and narrative-driven experiences.

Impact

  • It was also a rewarding experience as the stories and experiences I've contributed to reached 100s of students in Hyderabad.
  • 100s of students (from schools in Hyderabad) signed up for our after school hobby program.

Learning experiences

  • It was an exciting time as I could work and build on learning and research of Mitch Resnick and Seymour Papert, whom I admired.
  • We designed environments and experiences that encourage collaboration, tinkering etc.
  • Apart from skills, it also gave a heavy emphasis to building a community and addressed dispositions that hinder creative thinking.
  • We designed it as a maker-space and club, with regular challenges.

Game-like experience

  1. Role playing as Alivers; World Building
  • The students take up roles as Alivers. Their quest is for an immunity from turning into non-thinking zombies. The immunity lies in realizing the Aarx (seek, possibility, reality, play) which are various elements of creative thinking and making.

  • This Alivers manifesto : Various skills that students need to hone and practice.

  1. Narrative -
  • Every challenge has a short story. It creates a subtext of the skills and dispositions required to take up that challenge.
  • Some of the challenges
    • Students come up with riddles for a technology they use in their lives. They have to riddle their cohort.
    • Students pick a person card and a situation card. They have to put themselves in that person’s shoes in that situation and think of problems that they might face then.
    • Students observe and talk to people at home and their neighborhood. They ask them what problems they face in their everyday living and fill up a worksheet.
    • Coming up with Wacky inventions.

Maker Faire Hyderabad, 2018

Our studio was commissioned to design and execute the Kids Experience for MakerFaire Hyderabad, 2018. My contributions to the project were in conceptualizing and designing the experience and content design.

The exhibition space was transformed as a utopian city of makers, Alivers Metropolis.

The space showcased kids’ projects (in Seeker Street of the metropolis) from Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. It had spaces where kids could get a peek into aspects of design, creative thinking and making; through activities and workshops.
