Designing course discovery experience - Sulekha e-learn

About E-Learn

Sulekha e-learn, now called Capshine is a marketplace for online live learning. Students can approach a teacher on the platform and take classes with them, according to their learning needs and requirements. The platform encourages teachers to become entrepreneurs and run their classes.

My Role & Contribution

I’ve led the project, presented design artifacts to all stakeholders, gathered feedback and iterated.

Duration : 3 months

Team : Parshuram (Visual design), Akhil (UX Research), Saurabh (Principal Designer & Review)

In the first phase, we worked to design the course discovery & selection experience for learners.

Deliverables, Impact & Learnings

  • Delivered a synthesis document detailing how students search courses, choose. Their needs, concerns and various behaviors they exhibit while selecting.
  • Delivered a responsive Mobile and Web UX.
  • Even with these design interventions, students were having a tough time finding a teacher who meets their needs, and there was a lot of churn. The complexity in selecting a tutor should be further reduced. The next iteration should address this at a system level.

The Goal

How can we help students find the right teacher — among many offering on the platform — for their learning needs?
