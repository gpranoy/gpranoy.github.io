THE COMPANY

Indusface is a cybersecurity SaaS to protect apps and apis from security threats.

Security teams can easily detect vulnerabilities and protect by making virtual patches on the platform. Its integrated in 5000+ apps.

CHALLENGE & REQUIREMENT

Make the UX modern and self-serviced to enter global markets

Indusface was venturing to international markets with different needs, like self servicing, than their current Indian customers who liked their cybersecurity managed.

They wanted to make their design to compete with global competitors who had modern UX.

WE SET OUT TO

Understand customers requirements, identify issues at various levels and address them.

We spoke to customers who gave a walk through of the platform and told the major issues they face.

customer needs, the screens where the issue occurs, and the category of issue.

Let’s look at the issues and needs we identified, and how we addressed them. 👇🏽

ISSUE

1. The old Information Architecture reached a breaking point, with new and complex workflows

Indusface’s IA grew organically over time with new features and workflows, as customers grew. Force-fitting new workflows into the same IA made it tough to navigate the Jobs to be done (JTBDs.)

DESIGN RECOMMENDATION

We identified the Jobs to be done (JTBDs) of the personas and mapped the current paths. we identified how users didn’t resonate with the first click to start the path, in our user interviews.

We improved the flows improved on 3 parameters for all the major JTBDs

  • reduced the number of clicks
  • a meaningful first click
  • pathway to the next sub-task in the journey.

We reorganized information into meaningful categories

USER CONCERN

2. The information is heavy with numbers

It was difficult to identify anomalies, as one had to recollect rather than identify.

DESIGN RECOMMENDATION

Making numbers visual to identify anomalies easily.

We designed visualizations for various data information points.

USER NEED

3. Delving deep into the details of trends/summary

The trends and summaries are important for the Chief Security officer, however they’d also like to see the details for a few things that catch their eye. These details were in other pages with no link between them.

SOLUTION

Easy pathway from analytics to detailed lists

USER NEED

4. Correlating actions done on the platform to changes

While analyzing for anomalies, a view into the changes done on the platform is handy information. Without which one might conclude that a regular pattern as an anomaly
