1. Vision Definition

We had lengthy discussions with the founders. About the problems they want to address, what led them to think in those directions, etc. They were expressive and gave many examples, narrated stories, who their idols were and more. From the discussions, we looked for patterns.

We noticed 4 themes emerging. We shared those with Augli's team. After reviews and feedback with them and other stakeholders, We mutually decided to work on 4 themes.
